# Project: EPA QA Operating System

## Purpose
Operational framework for navigating EPA bureaucracy and producing scientific products in the Office of Applied Science and Environmental Solutions (formerly Office of Research and Development).

## Status
active

## Structure
```
docs/
  policies/      # Agency and office policies
  procedures/    # Administrative procedures
  qapps/         # Quality Assurance Project Plans
  sops/          # Standard Operating Procedures
  templates/     # Reusable document templates
research/        # Individual research subprojects
playbooks/       # Step-by-step guides for common tasks
contacts/        # Key contacts and organizational knowledge
```

## Research Subprojects
Individual research efforts live in `research/<project-name>/` with their own:
- QAPP (or reference to parent QAPP)
- SOPs specific to the effort
- Data management plan
- Deliverables tracking

## Key Concepts
- **QAPP**: Quality Assurance Project Plan - defines data quality objectives, methods, QC procedures
- **SOP**: Standard Operating Procedure - detailed step-by-step for specific tasks
- **QA/QC**: Quality Assurance (planning) / Quality Control (execution)

## Playbook Categories
- Publications & clearance process
- Procurement & contracts
- Data management & release
- Interagency collaboration
- Congressional/public inquiries
- Personnel & hiring
- Travel & conferences

## Current Focus
Initial setup - ingesting foundational documents and building institutional knowledge base

## Notes
- EPA requires documented QA for all research producing environmental data
- Clearance processes can be lengthy - plan ahead
- "Getting shit done" requires knowing which forms, who approves, and realistic timelines
