# SAP Meeting November 12, 2025 - Meeting Notes

**Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a0UCcZWx9ao
**Time Range:** 3:00 - 15:00

## Summary

This segment of the SAP (Shellfish Advisory Panel) meeting focuses on Province River (Area E) shellfish harvesting schedules. Anna presents a status update showing that 15 out of 23 potential harvest days were realized during Summer 2025, yielding 4.7 million clams in 45 hours of harvest. The meeting then moves to reviewing two industry proposals for the upcoming winter/spring schedule.

The Round Shell Fishermen's Association presents Proposal #1, which aims to expand access by switching from bi-weekly to weekly openings (Monday-Thursday first available) starting February 9th. The proposal includes adjusted opening times (9-11 AM in February due to late sunrise and weather concerns, 8-10 AM in March) while keeping 2-hour windows. If fully realized, the schedule would provide 24 total hours and an estimated 1.7 million clam harvest.

## Key Points

- Summer 2025 harvest: 15 of 23 days open, 4.7 million clams harvested, 45 hours realized
- 24 hours of scheduled harvest time went unused (one potential day remaining: Dec 12)
- Industry Proposal #1 changes from bi-weekly to weekly openings
- February openings shifted to 9-11 AM (from 8-10 AM) due to late sunrise (~6:50 AM) and icy conditions
- March openings return to 8-10 AM as sunrise moves earlier (~6:15-6:30 AM)
- Proposal avoids Friday first-available to prevent problematic Monday catch deliveries
- Last year's winter/spring default schedule achieved all 6 openings with lower attendance (~95 harvesters max)
- Winter rain closures have increased due to climate change (snow used to fall instead, which doesn't trigger closures)

## Action Items

- [ ] Round Shell Fishermen's Association to communicate time changes via their website
- [ ] DEM to update their website with schedule information
- [ ] Consider having DEM enforcement present early to remind harvesters of 9 AM start time in February
- [ ] Explore using closure hotline or other communication channels for schedule notifications
